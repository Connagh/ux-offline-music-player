# Privacy Policy for Offline Music Player

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement explains what personal data "Offline Music Player" collects from you, through our interactions with you and through our product, and how we use that data.

## 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**
"Offline Music Player" is designed to be a completely offline experience.
*   **No Accounts:** We do not require you to create an account or login.
*   **No Tracking:** We do not track your usage, location, or listening habits.
*   **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to collect data about how you use the app.

## 2. Your Music Library
Your music files and library data (playlists, favorites, listening history) are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive container. We (the developers) have no access to your files or your listening data. All processing of audio files (e.g., for Equalizer or Rhythm Mode) is performed locally on your device.

## 3. Payments
Our app offers a "Pro" mode via In-App Purchases.
*   All financial transactions are handled securely and directly by Apple via the App Store.
*   We do not process, store, or have access to your credit card information or billing details.
*   We only receive an anonymous confirmation from Apple if the "Pro" entitlement has been unlocked.
